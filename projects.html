I began this project to keep a portfolio of my work and to improve my web development skills. Briscola Statistics Tracker

This is a simple Android app that can elegantly record all the data pertaining to a single game of Briscola (a trick based card game similar to Euchre). It keeps track of who is playing, who won the bid, what card he or she called, and so on. The app also allows going back and changing information in an intuitive way. I started this app because of the folks over at Sony (SNEI) situated in Waterloo, Ontario, since they play this game everyday during lunch and I had a blast when I worked there. During this project, I learned about the Android activity life-cycle and how to create clean UI with XML.
