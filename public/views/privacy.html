Privacy Policy

I collect some anonymous information on the activity of the site's visitors. The purpose is mainly to see if there is any way to improve the site experience. I do not collect any personal identifiable information nor do I sell any information I gather to any third parties. Some sections of the site allow you to submit information (e.g. signing up or creating a League on My LoL Fantasy). This information is stored by the service in order to provide certain site functionality. You are welcome to use pseudonymous information in order to protect your identity.

Cookies

This site makes use of cookies to do a few things. One: I use Google Analytics to get an idea of the site's traffic. Two: certain sections of the site (My LoL Fantasy for instance) make use of a session identifier to keep track of session-based information on visitors, such as whether a user is logged in. That's pretty much it! Nothing too out of the ordinary.
